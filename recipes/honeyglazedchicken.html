Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

Honey Glazed Chicken is a delicious and easy-to-make dish with a sweet and savory glaze.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Honey
  • Soysauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the chicken until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and honey, and stir to combine.
  6. Simmer for 5-10 minutes or until the glaze is thickened.
